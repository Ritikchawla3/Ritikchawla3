E-Waste Management

At M/S SEMINOLE ELECTRONICS PVT LTD we understand that our responsibility doesn't end at selling you, our products. M/S SEMINOLE ELECTRONICS PVT LTDhas been working in the area of safe disposal of electronic waste. Ltd., to collect, dismantle and dispose e-waste - collected from all over India. Horizon Recycling Pvt.
